NUSA DUA, BALI

Aman Villas at Nusa Dua

On Bali's southernmost peninsula, famed for its golden beaches, ocean-side temples and frangipani-
scented hillsides, Aman Villas at Nusa Dua rest poolside in lush tropical gardens. The perfect starting point
for a journey through Indonesia, eachis an Eden-like retreat serviced by two butlers and a private chef, with
easy access to a private Beach Terrace.





ACCOMODATION

Villas

Tucked within fragrant gardens along rolling fairways that overlook the Indian ocean, the Villas are classically styled and finished with graceful Balinese touches.

DINING

Bespoke dining

Cuisine focuses on Balinese local produce and seafood from nearby
Jimbaran Market and can be tailored to guests personal requirements
and enjoyed in a number of private locations.

Getting Here

Aman Villas at Nusa Dua is a 20-minute drive from Denpasar Airport Transfers from the
airport to the property are complimentary.

Our multilingual reservations team is always available to help with travel planning, from booking a single
